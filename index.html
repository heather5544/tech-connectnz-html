How TechConnect Works

Our platform makes it easy to find the perfect mentor-mentee match.

1

Register Your Profile

Create your profile with professional skills and optional diversity information to help us find the best matches.

2

Browse Potential Matches

Our algorithm suggests potential mentors or mentees based on expertise, goals, and cultural context.

Connect & Grow

Celebrating Diversity in Tech

Our platform recognizes and values the diverse perspectives in Aotearoa's tech community.

🏮

Cultural Context

We incorporate Te Ao Māori and other cultural perspectives to create mentorship matches that respect and celebrate heritage.

🌏

Regional Connections

Whether you're in Auckland or Southland, we connect you with mentors and mentees who understand your regional context.

🌈

Gender & Identity

Our platform is specifically designed for women and non-binary individuals, with thoughtful consideration of diverse gender identities.

♿

Accessibility

We're committed to making tech mentorship accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities and different access needs.
