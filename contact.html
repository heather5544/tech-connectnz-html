Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry? +
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line during business hours.
I'm having technical issues with the platform. What should I do? +
For technical support, please email support@techconnect.nz with details of the issue you're experiencing, including any error messages and the device/browser you're using. Screenshots are helpful when applicable.
How can I provide feedback about the platform? +
We welcome feedback! You can use this contact form (select "Platform Feedback" as the subject), or email feedback@techconnect.nz directly. Your insights help us improve the TechConnect experience for everyone.
