1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

At TechConnect NZ, we believe that technology mentorship should be accessible to everyone, regardless of abilities or disabilities. We are committed to ensuring that our platform is usable by all women and non-binary individuals in the tech community, including those with disabilities.

Our approach to accessibility is guided by the principle of universal design – creating an environment that can be accessed, understood, and used by all people regardless of their age, size, ability, or disability.

We recognize that accessibility is particularly important in the tech industry, where people with disabilities are often underrepresented. By creating an accessible mentorship platform, we aim to contribute to a more diverse and inclusive tech ecosystem in Aotearoa New Zealand.

Our Vision: A tech industry where everyone has equal access to mentorship opportunities and career growth, regardless of ability status.

2. Accessibility Standards

We strive to ensure that our platform aligns with internationally recognized accessibility standards:

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, which cover a wide range of recommendations for making web content more accessible to people with various disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

New Zealand Government Web Accessibility Standard

We follow the NZ Government Web Accessibility Standard 1.1, which is based on WCAG 2.1 Level AA and provides additional guidance for the New Zealand context.

Ongoing Compliance Efforts

We regularly audit our platform against these standards and work continuously to address any issues identified. While we strive for full compliance, we acknowledge that we are on a journey of continuous improvement.

3. Accessibility Features

Our platform includes the following accessibility features:

🔍
Text Resizing

All text can be resized without loss of content or functionality, and the layout adapts accordingly.

🎨
Color Contrast

We maintain sufficient color contrast between text and background for better readability.

⌨️
Keyboard Navigation

All functionality is accessible using a keyboard, without requiring specific timings for individual keystrokes.

🔉
Screen Reader Support

Our content is structured for screen reader compatibility with proper headings, landmarks, and text alternatives.

📱
Responsive Design

Our platform adapts to different screen sizes and orientations, making it usable across devices.

📄
Structured Content

Proper headings, lists, and semantic markup help users navigate and understand content.

Alternative Communication in Mentorships

We support diverse communication preferences in mentorship relationships, including:

  • Text-based messaging for those who prefer or require written communication
  • Video calls with caption capabilities
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate different needs
  • Options to provide meeting notes or summaries

4. Accessibility Settings

Our platform offers personalized accessibility settings that you can adjust according to your preferences:

How to Access Accessibility Settings

  1. Log in to your TechConnect NZ account
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
  3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Navigate to the "Accessibility" tab

Available Settings

  • Text Size: Adjust the size of text throughout the platform
  • High Contrast Mode: Enable higher contrast colors for better visibility
  • Reduced Motion: Minimize animations and transitions
  • Screen Reader Optimization: Enhanced compatibility with screen readers
  • Keyboard Navigation Focus: More visible focus indicators for keyboard users

Accommodations for Mentorship

You can also specify accessibility needs related to mentorship interactions:

  • Preferred communication methods
  • Meeting format preferences (text, video, audio)
  • Need for captioning or transcription services
  • Specific accommodations for virtual or in-person meetings

Privacy Note: All accessibility preferences and accommodation requests are treated with respect and confidentiality. You control how much of this information is shared with potential mentors or mentees.
